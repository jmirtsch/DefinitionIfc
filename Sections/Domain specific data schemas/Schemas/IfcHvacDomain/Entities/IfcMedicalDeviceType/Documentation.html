The flow terminal type IfcMedicalDeviceType defines commonly shared information for occurrences of medical devices. The set of shared information may include:

  • common properties with shared property sets
  • common representations
  • common materials
  • common composition of elements
  • common ports

It is used to define a medical device type specification indicating the specific product information that is common to all occurrences of that product type. The IfcMedicalDeviceType may be declared within IfcProject or IfcProjectLibrary using IfcRelDeclares and may be exchanged with or without occurrences of the type. Occurrences of IfcMedicalDeviceType are represented by instances of IfcMedicalDevice. Refer to the documentation at IfcMedicalDevice for supported property sets, materials, composition, and ports.
